Anja Aurand

      Anja Aurand is a French/Swedish graphic designer educated at ENSAAMA (Paris) and the Gerrit Rietveld Academie (Amsterdam). Her practice engages with printed matter, language, performance, scale and collaborative practices — book-making, large-scale campaigns, installations, event curation and printmaking.
